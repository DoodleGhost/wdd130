Where Will You Go Next?

King River

Take a one day ride along the King River! Near West Coast Tasmania, tackle Grade 3 rapids while also viewing one of the world's oldest living organisms, the Huon Pine. Complete your journey with a ride on the West Coast Wilderness Railway's heritage steam train.

Tully River

Just a day trip from the Great Barrier Reef, the Tully River offers amazing Grade 3 and 4 rapids in tropical, warm water. Take a ride on the river that hosted the 2019 World Rafting Championships over 45 rapids that wind through beautiful rainforest.

Mitta Mitta River

Tackle 60 rapids graded 2 through 4, including the ominous "Graveyard" rapid, on our one or two day rafting adventures. Just 45 minutes from Mount Hotham, brace yourself for plenty of white water! Word to the wise: Bring a Jacket!
